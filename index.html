Every Day · A Gentle Rhythm

the shape of an ordinary day
Tap any task to see step-by-step instructions and tips
🌅

Morning

before 9:30 AM

  • Wake gently — be ready by 7:00 AM
  • Prepare and serve breakfast for Madam and Sir
  • Make Baby Azzam's breakfast (soft, safe food)
  • Pack Azzam's bag: clothes, diapers, wipes, snacks, water
  • Wash the breast pump parts and place them in Madam's bag
  • Pack Madam's lunchbox with last night's leftovers
  • Check the fridge — gently remove anything expired
  • Wash breakfast dishes and wipe the kitchen counter and stove
A note: please leave the living room to Sir in the morning.
☀️

Midday

9:30 AM — 5:00 PM

  • Quick wipe of all bathrooms
  • The day's main cleaning task (see weekly plan)
  • Laundry on the day it's scheduled (see laundry plan)
  • Hand-wash Azzam's clothes every two days, air dry only
  • Mop the kitchen and hallway floors
  • Take your own lunch from the leftovers
  • Tidy Azzam's play area in the middle of the day
  • Begin dinner prep by 6:00 PM
Take your time: do one thing well before the next.
🌙

Evening

5:00 PM onwards

  • Around 5 PM Azzam comes home — let his toys stay out
  • Dinner prep at 6:00 PM, served at 8:00 PM
  • Help or prepare dinner as Madam asks
  • Wipe the dining table and chairs after the meal
  • Wash dinner dishes, wipe stove and counters
  • Mop the kitchen floor
  • Empty kitchen and dining trash bins
  • Quick wipe of your own bathroom
After cleanup: the kitchen or your room is yours — please rest.

Laundry · A Plan for the Week

each load has its own day
every 2 days · by hand

Baby Azzam's Clothes

  • Wash by hand only — please don't use the machine
  • Use the gentle baby-safe detergent only
  • Rinse thoroughly — no soap residue
  • Air dry only — never the dryer
  • Fold and arrange in Azzam's area
  • Suggested days: Sun, Tue, Thu, Sat
Wednesday

Adults — Whites

  • Whites only — please don't mix with darks
  • Machine wash — warm cycle
  • Dry, iron, and fold neatly
  • Arrange in the wardrobe by type
Thursday

Adults — Darks

  • Darks only — please keep apart from whites
  • Machine wash — cold cycle
  • Dry, iron, and fold neatly
  • Arrange in the wardrobe by type
Friday or as needed

Colored Home Clothes

  • Home clothes (not white or black) — may be washed together
  • No strict separation needed for these
  • Machine wash — cold or warm cycle
  • Fold and put away neatly
as needed

Light Going-Out Clothes

  • If only a few pieces, please wash by hand
  • If using the dryer: lowest, lightest setting only
  • Handle gently — these are delicate
  • Hang or fold carefully after drying
  • Always check the label first
Friday

Towels & Bed Linens

  • Machine wash — regular cycle
  • Dry fully before folding
  • Iron and fold the linens neatly
  • Store in their proper place
Saturday or as needed

Sophia's Own Clothes

  • You wash your own clothes separately
  • Please keep apart from family laundry
  • Use weekend time for this
always

Gentle Rules

  • Whites and darks stay apart
  • Iron and fold before placing in the wardrobe
  • Please don't leave wet clothes in the machine
  • Start the machine early in the morning when you can

Bathrooms · A Simple Care Plan

a quick daily touch + a deeper weekly turn

🚿 Every Day · All Bathrooms

  • Wipe sink and tap — gently remove water marks
  • Wipe toilet seat and the outside of the bowl
  • Quick rinse of the floor if needed
  • Hang towels neatly
  • A small spray of freshener if there is any smell

✨ Weekly Deep Turn

  • Sunday: Your own bathroom — full scrub (tiles, toilet inside, sink, mirror, floor)
  • Tuesday: Your own bathroom — full scrub again (a midweek reset)
  • Wednesday: Storage bathroom — light clean and tidy
  • Saturday: Private bathroom — full clean after the family wakes (~11 AM)
  • Weekdays: please enter the private bathroom only when the family is home
06

Monthly · Deep Care

once a month, a slower, deeper round

🍳 Kitchen Deep Clean

  • Full fridge clean — empty, wipe every shelf and drawer
  • Clean inside all kitchen cabinets
  • Degrease the oven fully — racks included
  • Descale kettle and coffee maker
  • Clean behind fridge and under all appliances
  • Wash the bin with disinfectant inside and outside

🪟 Surfaces & Windows

  • Clean all exterior windows with glass cleaner
  • Wipe all skirting boards and baseboards
  • Clean A/C vents and ceiling fans
  • Wipe walls in high-traffic areas
  • Wash curtains or wipe blinds

🛁 Deep Bathroom Scrub

  • Scrub all tiles and grout in every bathroom
  • Descale showerhead and taps
  • Clean behind toilet and under sink
  • Wash bathroom mats and rugs
  • Sanitize all bathroom accessories and holders

📦 Storage & Toys

  • Sort and reorganize camping/entry storage
  • Rotate seasonal items in the storage bathroom
  • Full toy audit — clean, sort, store unused toys
  • Check the pantry — discard expired items
  • Wipe all storage shelves
